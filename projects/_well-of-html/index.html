Lithuanian cherries

Why cherries are so popular in Lithuania?

There are over few hundred types of cherry-trees growing in Lithuania.
Some cherries are sour and some are sweet.
There are even species which grow in a form of a bush.
Some cherries have a fuzzy texture just like peaches.

Most popular types of cheery-trees

  • Trees
  • Bushes
  • Mini-trees

Most popular types of cherries

  1. Sweet dark-red cherries
  2. Semi-sweet yellow cherries
  3. Fuzzy cherries
  4. Sour pink cherries
